Bhari Bisaunu

Bhari Bisaunu is Nepali term for resting your load. Why would you want to take a rest knowing that you have to pick it up again and keep on going until you reach your destination. Obviously because a short break will give you more energy, reinvigorate you for the rest of the journey. Then why don’t we prioritize taking break from work?
